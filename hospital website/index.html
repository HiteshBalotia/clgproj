about us

we take care of your healthy life

Our Prime Minister’s clarion call for ‘Heal in India’ is a great endorsement and a signal that the world needs a new vision for healthcare. A system that is holistic. A system that is proactive and a system that optimises quality of life. We believe India, with its heritage of holistic healthcare, can take the lead in providing this new system of care to the world. Patients from over 150 countries come to India for treatment that is at par with western countries at a fraction of the cost. With the ‘Heal in India’ initiative, We are sure we will have the opportunity to serve many more people from around the world right here in India.

Our comprehensive diagnostics, technology-aided consultations with eminent doctors and quality healthcare at affordable cost are some of the many reasons our hospital is every patient's first choice.
